Southeast Michigan April 2015 Market Update

During the April Market Update, Dan discusses what went on in the Southeast Michigan real estate market during the first quarter of 2015. There was a rush of sales as well as new listings coming onto the market, particularly in March. Dan talks about the market conditions within various prices ranges, and also lets us know what's to come for the summer and the second half of the year.
